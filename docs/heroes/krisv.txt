KRISV
The Deadeye Marksman

Tier: Premium
Unlock: $2.99 or 2500 Essence
Difficulty: Expert
Class: Ranger (Sharpshooter)

LORE

KrisV hails from the distant Iron Isles, a land of black powder and mechanical innovation. Once the Isles' greatest marksman, a tragic mistake led him to vow only to hunt true monsters with his rebuilt rifle.

"One shot. That's all I need. That's all I ever needed."

BASE STATS

- HP: 104
- ATK: 12
- DEF: 7
- Mana: 10 / 60 (Regen: 4/turn)
- Crit Chance: 20%
- Crit Multiplier: 1.75x
- DEF Penetration: 0%
- Dodge: 8%
- Focus Meter: 20 / 120

PASSIVE - SHARPSHOOTER'S FOCUS

Gain Focus each turn. High Focus empowers abilities.

Focus Gains:
- +5 Focus per Attack or Ability.
- +20 Focus if turn is skipped.
- +20 Focus on Kill.
- -5 Focus when damaged (once per turn).

Focus Thresholds:
- 50+ Focus: +10% Crit Chance.
- 75+ Focus: +15% Crit Chance and +10% ATK.
- 100+ Focus: +20% Crit Chance and +20% ATK.

Scaling:
- For every 100 Focus consumed, KrisV gains 2-8 permanent ATK.

ABILITY 1 - PRECISE SHOT

High damage shot. Stronger with Focus.
- Cost: 12 / 15 / 18 / 21 / 24 / 27 / 30 Mana
- Cooldown: 2 turns
- Damage: 160% ATK, ignoring 30-60% enemy DEF.
- 75+ Focus: Damage increases to 200% ATK.
- 100+ Focus: Damage increases to 240% ATK and consumes 50 Focus.

ABILITY 2 - FLASHBANG GRENADE

Blind enemy and gain Focus.
- Cost: 16 / 20 / 24 / 28 / 32 / 36 / 40 Mana
- Cooldown: 3 turns
- Effect: Enemy gains Blinded for 2 turns (50% accuracy loss).
- Focus: KrisV gains 20-50 Focus.
- 100+ Focus: Deals 70-130% ATK, Blinded lasts 3 turns, and consumes 25 Focus.

ABILITY 3 - PERFECT SHOT (ULTIMATE)

True damage sniper shot.
- Cost: 30 / 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 Mana
- Cooldown: 5 turns
- Damage: 300-480% ATK as TRUE DAMAGE (cannot miss).
- 75+ Focus: Damage increases to 400-640% ATK.
- 100+ Focus: Damage increases to 500-800% ATK.
- Effect: Heals for 25% of damage dealt.
- On Execute: Gains 3-21 permanent Max HP and consumes all Focus.
