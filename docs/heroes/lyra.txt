PASSIVE - ARCANE MOMENTUM

Consecutive spells grow stronger. Basic attacks reset the chain but restore mana.

- Momentum: Each consecutive ability used grants 1 Momentum stack (max 3).
- 1 Stack: +10% ability damage.
- 2 Stacks: +20% ability damage.
- 3 Stacks: +30% ability damage and spells ignore 15% DEF.
- Reset: Using a basic attack resets Momentum to 0. If resetting from max stacks, Lyra restores 3-21 Mana.

ABILITY 1 - FIREBOLT

Quick fire spell. Burns enemy over time.
- Cost: 10 / 12 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 20 / 22 Mana
- Cooldown: 1 turn
- Damage: 100 / 120 / 140 / 160 / 180 / 200 / 220% ATK.
- Effect: Applies Burn for 3 turns (5% enemy Max HP per turn).
- Synergy: If enemy is already Burning, Firebolt heals Lyra for 20-50% damage dealt.
- On Kill: Regains 4 / 6 / 8 / 10 / 12 / 14 / 16 Mana.

ABILITY 2 - FROST BARRIER

Shield that absorbs damage. Shatters to damage and slow enemy.
- Cost: 14 / 17 / 20 / 23 / 26 / 29 / 32 Mana
- Cooldown: 3 turns
- Effect: Absorbs up to 50% Max HP in damage for 2 turns.
- Shatter: When it breaks/expires, it deals 60-120% ATK to the enemy and applies Chilled (enemy deals 15% less damage for 2 turns).

ABILITY 3 - PYROCLASM

Massive fire burst. Stronger against Burning enemies.
- Cost: 28 / 32 / 36 / 40 / 44 / 48 / 52 Mana
- Cooldown: 4 turns
- Damage: 180 / 210 / 240 / 270 / 300 / 330 / 360% ATK.
- Synergy: If enemy is Burning, consumes the Burn to deal 100% of remaining Burn damage instantly.
- Scaling: Each cast grants 2-14 permanent ATK and Max HP.

PLAYSTYLE SUMMARY

Lyra is a technical caster who rewards turn planning to maintain Arcane Momentum.

- Combat Flow: Avoid basic attacks unless mana is critical, as resetting Momentum loses significant damage. Aim for 3 stacks for the DEF penetration.
- Scaling Strategy: Lyra is a Battle-Mage. Pyroclasm grants permanent stats with every cast, rewarding players who manage her mana well.
- Shop Priority: Max HP is critical (Frost Barrier scales with Max HP). ATK is necessary to end fights before her mana regen is overwhelmed.
