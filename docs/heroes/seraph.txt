Stat Scaling:
- Lunaris benefits from 50% of all permanent stat gains Seraph receives from items and shops.

PASSIVE - CONSTELLATION BOND

Lunaris assists Seraph's attacks. Build Starlight Charges to empower abilities.

- Lunaris Assist: 75% chance for Lunaris to attack for 100% ATK whenever Seraph damages an enemy.
- Safeguard: When Seraph is below 70% HP, Lunaris always assists and heals Seraph for 15-45% of damage dealt.
- Starlight Charges: Gain 1 Charge per ability used (max 3).
- Empowerment: At 3 Charges, Lunaris assists for 120% ATK. Consuming 3 Charges grants Lunaris 2-8 permanent ATK.

ABILITY 1 - CELESTIAL ARROW

Starlight shot. Lunaris may assist. Stronger at 2+ charges.
- Cost: 16 / 20 / 24 / 28 / 32 / 36 / 40 Mana
- Cooldown: 2 turns
- Damage: 140-260% ATK.
- Effect: Triggers Lunaris Assist.
- Charge Synergy: At 2+ Starlight Charges, ignores 40% enemy DEF.
- On Kill: Serapha gains 1-13 permanent ATK.

ABILITY 2 - ASTRAL HOWL

Debuff enemy, buff self, grow stronger.
- Cost: 22 / 26 / 30 / 34 / 38 / 42 / 46 Mana
- Cooldown: 4 turns
- Debuff: Enemy takes 20% extra damage from Seraph and Lunaris for 3 turns.
- Buff: Seraph gains 10% Dodge and 20% Crit Chance for 3 turns.
- Scaling: At the end of the buff, Seraph gains 2-8 permanent Max HP.
- Charge Synergy: At 3 Starlight Charges, consumes them to heal Seraph for 20-35% Max HP and Lunaris to full.

ABILITY 3 - STARFALL REQUIEM (ULTIMATE)

Devastating star volley.
- Cost: 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 Mana
- Cooldown: 5 turns
- Damage: Fires 4 arrows, each dealing 70-100% ATK. Each ignores 30% DEF and can crit.
- Lunaris Synergy: Lunaris charges for 50% ATK per Starlight Charge.
- Charge Synergy: At 3 Starlight Charges, on execute, creates a zone that deals 10-16% Max HP damage to the next enemy.

STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES

Strengths:
- High total damage output (Serapha + Lunaris).
- Built-in sustain and infinite scaling.
- Strong buff/debuff synergy.

Weaknesses:
- Very fragile baseline stats.
- Significant damage loss if Lunaris dies.
- High mana costs and complex resource management.
